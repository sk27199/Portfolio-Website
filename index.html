About Me

Hi, I’m Shrish. I’m an aspiring Software and Cyber Security Apprentice with a strong interest in how applications are built and how they can be made secure. I have a background in Business and Management and I’m currently developing my technical skills through self-study and hands-on projects. I mainly work with Python, where I’ve built projects covering both software development and security-focused concepts, including API-based applications, database-driven systems using SQL, and security tools such as a port scanner and a password hashing system. Through these projects, I’ve developed an interest in writing clean, reliable code while also understanding security risks, secure data handling, and ethical use of tools. I enjoy breaking problems down into simple steps and improving my solutions as I learn more. I’m currently applying for Level 4 and Degree Apprenticeships in Software Engineering and Cyber Security, where I can gain real-world experience, learn from experienced professionals, and continue developing both my development and security skills.
